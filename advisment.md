## CIS Advisement Center

Our advisors work with liberal arts, non-degree, exploratory, and pre-health students to develop personalized goals and an educational plan.

### Who We Serve
* **Exploratory Students:** Not sure of your major? We help you navigate UNM's offerings.
* **Interdisciplinary Studies Majors:** Support for students in the BA-IS (formerly LAIS) program.
* **Pre-Health Professions:** Specialized guidance for students aiming for medical, dental, or veterinary school.
* **Non-Degree Students:** Strategic planning for lifelong learners.

### Schedule an Appointment
