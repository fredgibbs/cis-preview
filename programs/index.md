## Diverse Pathways, One College

The College of Interdisciplinary Studies hosts programs that bridge the gap between traditional disciplines.

### Interdisciplinary Studies Program (formerly LAIS)
The **Bachelor of Arts in Interdisciplinary Studies** (BA-IS) allows students to design a unique program of study combining courses from two or more UNM departments. It is ideal for students with career aspirations that require expertise across multiple subjects.
* **Learn More:** [lais.unm.edu](https://lais.unm.edu)

### Academic Communities
Serving as the academic gateway to UNM, Academic Communities provide first-year students with the tools and peer networks needed to succeed in higher education.
* **Explore Communities:** [firstyear.unm.edu](https://firstyear.unm.edu)

### Global & National Security Studies
Through the **Global & National Security Policy Institute (GNSPI)**, we offer concentrations and certificates focused on intelligence, cybersecurity, and strategic analysis.
* **Program Details:** [gnspi.unm.edu](https://gnspi.unm.edu)

### Pre-Health Professions
A dedicated concentration and development office that helps students understand the intricacies of a successful application to health profession programs.
* **Guidance:** [Pre-Health Information](https://ucollege.unm.edu/programs/pre-health.html)

### Military Studies (ROTC)
CIS provides the administrative home for the Air Force, Army, and Naval ROTC programs, allowing students to work toward a commission while completing their degree.

### Security Studies
Interdisciplinary certificates and courses designed to prepare students for careers in the U.S. Intelligence Community and international NGOs.
